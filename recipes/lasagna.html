Lasagna

This delicious beauty will only take you 20mins of prep time with a maximum of 50mins cook time and can serve up to 4 people

INGREDIENTS

  • San Remo Reg No. 103 instant lasagna 250g
  • Parmesan chessse (to sprinkle on top before baking)

MEAT SAUCE

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • 500g beef mince
  • 5 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 x 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • salt and pepped, to taste
  • Pinch of nutmeg

CHEESE SAUCE

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 125g mozzarella or cheddar cheese

METHOD

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C.
  2. To make the meat sauce, heat oil in a saucepan. Add onion, carrot and garlic, cook until soft. Add beef mince, cook until brown.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes and chicken stock. Season width salt, pepper and nutmeg. Cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. To make the cheese sauce, melt butter in a separate saucepan, stir in flour and cook for 2 minutes over gentle heat.
  5. Remove from heat and gradually stir in the milk. Return to heat, and conrinue to stir wintil thick and smooth.
  6. Season with salt. pepper and add mozzarella or cheddar cheese, stirring over low heat until cheese melts.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, pour 1/2 cup of the meat sauce into a lightly greased baking dish. Alternate layers of lasagna, meat and cheese sauces, ending with cheese sauce layer (allow for 4 layers).
  8. Sprinkle with grated parmeasan cheese and bake for 35-40 minutes or until cooked through (test if cooked by pricking with fork).
  9. Allow lasana to stand for 5-10 minutes fbefore serving.
  10. Serve and enjoy with family and friends!
